# Design System Narrative

## Visual Identity
This design system is built on the principles of clarity, professional efficiency, and modern utility. By utilizing a unified sans-serif typographic approach and a focused monochrome palette, the interface provides a reliable and undistracted environment for users. The aesthetic is clean and contemporary, balancing accessibility with a structured corporate feel.

## Color Palette
The color strategy employs a monochrome blue foundation to convey trust and stability, accented by neutral tones for structural clarity.
- **Primary Color**: #007BFF (Classic Blue) serves as the primary driver for interactivity and brand recognition.
- **Neutral/Supporting Tones**: A consistent grey (#777777) is used across secondary and tertiary elements to maintain a low-friction visual hierarchy, ensuring that the primary blue remains the focal point for all critical actions.

## Typography
The system adopts **Public Sans** as its universal typeface across all roles—headlines, body text, and labels.
- **Modern Clarity**: Public Sans provides a neutral, yet friendly tone that excels in digital legibility.
- **Unity**: Using a single font family creates a cohesive experience, simplifying the visual landscape and emphasizing content over decorative flair.

## Shape & Spacing
The interface geometry is designed for balance and standardized usability.
- **Roundedness**: At a level 2 (Moderate), UI components feature clearly defined, softened corners. This provides an approachable feel without veering into the playfulness of pill-shaped designs or the severity of sharp edges.
- **Spacing**: A level 2 (Normal) spacing scale is utilized to create a familiar, standard density. This ensures that information is well-organized and breathable without being overly sparse or excessively cramped, making it ideal for professional applications and productivity tools.
